Paloma

What Is A Paloma?

Paloma is a delicious tequila cocktail that is a favorite in Mexico. It's a light and fruity drink with fizz — a great alternative to a margarita.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces tequila
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 cubes ice
  • 6 fluid ounces diet grapefruit soda

Steps

  1. Combine the tequila, lime juice, and salt in a tall glass.
  2. Add ice, top off with diet grapefruit soda, and stir.
